About

Personal Story: As a student at Holberton School, I embarked on the journey of creating the Currency Converter project to develop my skills in web development and provide a valuable tool to the community. Inspired by the need for a simple yet effective currency conversion solution, this project allowed me to combine my passion for coding with practical real-world application.

This project is not just about meeting academic requirements; it's a testament to my dedication and growth as a developer during my time at Holberton School.

Portfolio Project: The Currency Converter is part of my portfolio for Holberton School, showcasing my abilities and commitment to delivering high-quality software solutions. Thank you for visiting and exploring this project with me.
